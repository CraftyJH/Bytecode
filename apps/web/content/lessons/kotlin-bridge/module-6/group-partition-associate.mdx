**`groupBy`**: `List<T>` → `Map<K, List<T>>`.

**`partition`**: split into pass/fail buckets.

**`associate` / `associateWith` / `associateBy`**: build maps from collection elements.

## Recap

- **`groupBy`** answers “give me buckets”.
- **`partition`** answers “two piles: yes/no”.
