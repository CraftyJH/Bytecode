You will combine **data classes**, **extensions**, and **stdlib aggregation** to total quantities in a toy shopping cart — the kind of logic that sits inside domain services in Java, expressed with less ceremony in Kotlin.

## Specification

- **`Line`** carries **`sku`** and **`qty`**.
- **`totalUnits`** sums **`qty`** across all lines.
- **`main`** prints the aggregate for the sample cart (`A×2`, `B×3` → **`5`**).

## Stretch goals (offline)

- Filter lines by SKU prefix.
- Group totals by SKU using `groupingBy`.
- Parse lines from `stdin` instead of literals.
